## Hardware

💻  [Apple MacBook Pro 13-Inch 2017](https://www.pcmag.com/reviews/apple-macbook-pro-13-inch-2017). Because macbooks are the best machines ever made, change my mind.

🖥 🖥  [Dell P2419H](https://www.amazon.com/Dell-Screen-LED-Lit-Monitor-P2419H/dp/B07F8XZN69) + [Samsung C24F390](https://www.amazon.com/Samsung-LC24F390FHNXZA-24-inch-Monitor-FreeSync/dp/B01CX26WPY) Monitor's. Dell's monitor is the best! Curved monitors are overrated.

⌨️  [Keychron K8](https://www.keychron.com/products/keychron-k8-tenkeyless-wireless-mechanical-keyboard). Because mechanical. Do I need to say anything else?

🖱  [Apple Magic Mouse](https://www.amazon.com/-/es/Apple-Magic-Mouse-inal%C3%A1mbrico-recargable/dp/B07BR94PPD). And yes, I charge it time to times in that annoying way... the worst Apple design ever.

🔊  [Bose Companion 2 Series III Speakers](https://www.bose.com/en_us/products/speakers/stereo_speakers/companion-2-series-iii-multimedia-speaker-system.html). I really love the sound of this speakers.

🎤  [Samson Go Mic Connect](http://www.samsontech.com/samson/products/microphones/usb-microphones/gomicconnect/). Not bad for a USB mic.

🎥  [Logitech C920 Pro HD Webcam](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B085TFF7M1/). Solid quality for less than a hundred bucks.

🎚️  [Tobenone UCB-C Dock Station](https://www.amazon.com/Estaci%C3%B3n-acoplamiento-Monitor-pantalla-Ethernet/dp/B088D5436G?ref_=ast_sto_dp). This is my secret to connect everything without problems.

🖊️  [Apple Mini + Apple Pencil](https://www.apple.com/ipad-mini/). iPad's are the best table to take notes, drawing my talks and read some book works perfectly.

💺  [Ergohuman Fit Mesh](https://bookstore.cl/silla-ergohuman/silla-ergohuman-fit-mesh-58.html). After 5 unsuccessful purchases of  *gamer* chairs. The best is an ergonomic chair and this is the best.

👨‍💻  [Ergonomic Stand desk](https://ergonomics.cl/products/escritorio-electrico-avanzado?variant=36498974441621). Because stand-up meetings should be stand-up.

📱  [iPhone 12 Pro Max](https://www.apple.com/iphone-12-pro/). I love the 13mm lens.

📸  [Canon Rebel EOS T5](https://www.cla.canon.com/cla/es/support/consumer/cameras/eos_cameras/eos_rebel_t5). My first camera, I love Canon.

📸  [Sony Aplha Nex-5T](https://www.amazon.com/Sony-NEX-5TL-Mirrorless-Digital-16-50mm/dp/B00ENZRP38). My latest immersion in mirrorless cameras, an amazing one.
